Thor Odinson

Bio

Full Name: Thor Odinson

Alias(es): God of Thunder, Prince of Asgard

Species: Asgardian

Gender: Male

Age: 1500+ years

Affiliation: Avengers, Asgardian Royal Family

First Appearance: Journey into Mystery #83 (1962)

Creator: Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Jack Kirby

Backstory: Thor is the Norse God of Thunder, protector of the Nine Realms and wielder of Mjolnir. He evolves from a brash warrior to a wise hero through loss, battle, and redemption.

Abilities & Powers

Ability Description
Superhuman Strength Can lift over 100 tons and battle cosmic beings.
Lightning Control Summons and manipulates lightning.
Flight Flies using Mjolnir's momentum.
Weather Manipulation Can conjure storms and wind.
Immortality Extremely long lifespan, immune to aging and disease.
Combat Mastery Skilled in Asgardian warfare and weaponry.
